Methenolone enanthate, known as Primobolan Depot, is a DHT-derived anabolic steroid prized for its favorable side effect profile and ability to produce quality lean tissue gains. The 1-methyl and 1-en double bond modifications confer anabolic activity while keeping androgenic effects moderate. Methenolone does not aromatize and is considered one of the milder injectable compounds, making it popular for longer cycles and among users prioritizing health markers.

The enanthate ester provides a half-life of approximately 7 days, supporting once or twice-weekly injection schedules. A default dose of 200 mg per week is conservative; effective doses typically range from 400–600 mg weekly, though methenolone is often noted to require higher mg amounts relative to more potent compounds to achieve comparable results. Gains tend to be gradual and maintainable, with minimal water retention or bloating.

Methenolone is one of the few anabolic steroids that has been studied in clinical settings at relatively high doses with tolerable side effect profiles. It does suppress endogenous testosterone, though often to a lesser degree than more androgenic compounds. Hair loss and acne are possible given its DHT origin but occur less frequently than with stronger androgens. Detection time is approximately 4 months.
