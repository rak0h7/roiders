Drostanolone propionate, commonly known as Masteron, is a DHT-derived anabolic steroid with a 2-methyl group that enhances its anabolic properties while maintaining a relatively low androgenic profile. It does not aromatize to estrogen and in fact exhibits mild anti-estrogenic qualities through competitive inhibition at the receptor level. Masteron is primarily used during cutting phases to enhance muscle hardness, definition, and vascularity.

The propionate ester yields a half-life of approximately 2 days, requiring injections every other day or three times per week. A default dose of 100 mg every other day (350 mg per week) is standard, though doses up to 500 mg weekly are sometimes employed. The compound is most visually effective at lower body fat percentages, typically below 10–12%, where its cosmetic effects on muscle density become most apparent.

As a DHT derivative, drostanolone can accelerate hair loss in individuals genetically predisposed to male pattern baldness and may cause prostate enlargement symptoms. It provides minimal mass-building benefit compared to other anabolics and is rarely used as a primary compound in bulking cycles. Detection time is approximately 3 months, and the short ester allows relatively quick clearance compared to longer-acting injectables.
